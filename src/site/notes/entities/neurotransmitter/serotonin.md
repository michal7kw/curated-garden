<h1>Serotonin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Monoamine neurotransmitter regulating mood, sleep, appetite, and gut function. Approximately 95% is produced in the gut. Commonly known as the &quot;feel-good&quot; neurotransmitter, it is central to emotional well-being and is the target of most antidepressant medications.</p>
