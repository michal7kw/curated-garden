<h1>Pyruvate Dehydrogenase</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Large multi-enzyme complex that bridges glycolysis and the TCA cycle by irreversibly converting pyruvate to acetyl-CoA, CO2, and NADH. A critical metabolic gateway controlling the entry of carbohydrate-derived carbon into mitochondrial oxidative metabolism.</p>
