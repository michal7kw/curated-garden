<h1>Gut Health</h1>
<p>Restore microbiome diversity and gut barrier integrity.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Probiotics have strong evidence for IBS, antibiotic-associated diarrhea, and immune support. L-Glutamine is well-supported for intestinal barrier repair. Fiber (prebiotics) consistently shown to increase beneficial Bifidobacteria and butyrate production. Zinc carnosine has specific evidence for gastric mucosal healing.</p>
