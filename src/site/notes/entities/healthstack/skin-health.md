<h1>Skin Health</h1>
<p>Support collagen synthesis, antioxidant defense, and skin aging protection.</p>
<h2>Evidence Summary</h2>
<p>Collagen peptides have consistent evidence for improved skin elasticity and hydration (12-week studies). Vitamin C is essential for collagen synthesis and photoprotection. Astaxanthin shows UV-protective effects in RCTs. Omega-3 reduces UV-induced inflammation. Zinc supports wound healing and sebum regulation.</p>
