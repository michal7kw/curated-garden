<h1>Tongkat Ali</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Root extract of Eurycoma longifolia, a Southeast Asian medicinal plant traditionally used as an aphrodisiac and energy tonic. Contains bioactive quassinoids (eurycomanone) that modulate testosterone via HPA axis and SHBG mechanisms. Clinical evidence supports modest testosterone elevation, improved stress hormones, and ergogenic benefits.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>SHBG reduction</strong>: Frees bound testosterone, increasing bioavailable testosterone</li>
<li><strong>HPA axis modulation</strong>: Lowers cortisol, improving cortisol-to-testosterone ratio</li>
<li><strong>Aromatase inhibition</strong>: Eurycomanone inhibits CYP19, reducing estrogen conversion</li>
<li><strong>Leydig cell activation</strong>: Stimulates testicular steroidogenesis</li>
<li><strong>PDE inhibition</strong>: Contributes to vasodilation and ergogenic effects</li>
</ul>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Standardized extract</strong>: 100-200mg daily (1-2% eurycomanone)</li>
<li><strong>Hot water extract</strong>: 200-400mg daily</li>
<li><strong>Timing</strong>: Morning, with or without food</li>
<li><strong>Cycling</strong>: Some protocols recommend 5 days on / 2 days off</li>
</ul>
