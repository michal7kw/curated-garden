<h1>Cordyceps</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Medicinal mushroom traditionally used for energy and endurance. Supports ATP production, oxygen utilization, and exercise performance. Contains cordycepin with anti-inflammatory and adaptogenic properties.</p>
<p><strong>Research Summary:</strong> Traditional use for fatigue and respiratory issues. Modern research shows benefits for exercise performance, especially in trained individuals. Cordyceps militaris is more sustainable and may be more potent than wild C. sinensis.</p>
