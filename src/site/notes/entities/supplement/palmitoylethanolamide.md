<h1>Palmitoylethanolamide</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Endogenous fatty acid amide produced naturally in the body as part of the endocannabinoid system. Acts primarily through PPAR-alpha activation and indirect cannabinoid receptor modulation. Clinically studied for chronic pain, neuroinflammation, and neuroprotection. Available as a supplement in micronized (m-PEA) and ultra-micronized (um-PEA) forms for enhanced bioavailability.</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>PPAR-alpha activation</strong>: Reduces NF-kB-mediated inflammatory gene expression</li>
<li><strong>Entourage effect</strong>: Inhibits FAAH enzyme, increasing endogenous anandamide levels</li>
<li><strong>Mast cell modulation</strong>: Downregulates mast cell degranulation (ALIA mechanism)</li>
<li><strong>Microglial modulation</strong>: Reduces neuroinflammatory microglial activation</li>
<li><strong>TLR4 signaling</strong>: Modulates toll-like receptor 4 inflammatory cascade</li>
</ul>
<h2>Dosage</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Standard</strong>: 300-600mg twice daily</li>
<li><strong>Chronic pain</strong>: 600mg twice daily for 4-8 weeks</li>
<li><strong>Form</strong>: Ultra-micronized (um-PEA) preferred for bioavailability</li>
<li><strong>Duration</strong>: Benefits typically seen within 2-4 weeks</li>
</ul>
