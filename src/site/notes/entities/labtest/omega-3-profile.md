<h1>Omega-3 Profile</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Comprehensive fatty acid profile measuring the major omega-3 species (EPA, DHA, ALA, DPA) and key omega-6 species (arachidonic acid, linoleic acid) in red blood cell membranes or plasma. Used to derive several derived markers:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Omega-3 Index</strong> = (EPA + DHA) as % of total RBC fatty acids. Target: ≥8%; &lt;4% is associated with elevated CV risk (Harris &amp; von Schacky 2004).</li>
<li><strong>Arachidonic Acid : EPA ratio</strong> — proxy for inflammatory tone.</li>
<li><strong>Omega-6 : Omega-3 ratio</strong> — historically targeted ≤4:1, though modern evidence emphasizes absolute EPA+DHA over ratios.</li>
</ul>
<p>Common branded versions include Quest OmegaCheck, LabCorp Omega-3 Index Plus, and OmegaQuant Omega-3 Index. Most use HS-GC analysis on RBC membranes (more stable than plasma, reflects 4-month average intake).</p>
