<h1>Omega-3: DHA</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>oh-MAY-guh three: D-H-A Docosahexaenoic acid (DHA) is a long-chain omega-3 fatty acid essential for brain, eye, and nervous system function. It supports cognitive and visual health and helps maintain the structure and flexibility of cell membranes. Measuring DHA offers insight into omega-3 intake and balance, which play an important role in long-term brain and metabolic health.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
