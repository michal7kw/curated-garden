<h1>HDL Cholesterol</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Tested twice a year Think of the H in HDL as healthy, helpful, heart-supporting cholesterol. HDL's primary job is to remove excess cholesterol from the blood and transport it to the liver to be eliminated, reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease.</p>
<p><strong>Evidence Level:</strong> ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)</p>
