<h1>alzheimer_s_risk_apoe</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>This test looks at genetic variations in apolipoprotein E (ApoE), a protein essential for transporting cholesterol and regulating fat metabolism. Specific ApoE gene variations are associated with an increased risk of cardiovascular disease and late-onset Alzheimer’s. Identifying your ApoE genetic profile offers valuable knowledge of your inherited predisposition. It can guide targeted diet and lifestyle strategies to help reduce the risk of onset or related complications, such as heart attacks and strokes. Included biomarkers (1)</p>
