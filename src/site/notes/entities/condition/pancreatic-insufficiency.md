<h1>Pancreatic Insufficiency</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Exocrine Pancreatic Insufficiency (EPI) results in the inability to digest fats, proteins, and carbohydrates. This leads to <strong>Malabsorption</strong> and chronic diarrhea. It is a common long-term complication of chronic inflammation of the pancreas.</p>
<h2>Complications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Malnutrition.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Osteoporosis:</strong> Due to Vitamin D malabsorption.</li>
<li><strong>Sarcopenia:</strong> Due to protein malabsorption.</li>
</ul>
