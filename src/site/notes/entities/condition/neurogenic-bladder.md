<h1>Neurogenic Bladder</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Neurogenic Bladder occurs when the nerves that carry messages between the bladder and the brain/spinal cord are damaged. This can cause the bladder to become overactive (spastic) or underactive (flaccid). Proper management is critical to prevent permanent <strong>Kidney Damage</strong>.</p>
<h2>Complications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Hydronephrosis:</strong> Urine backup into kidneys.</li>
<li><strong>Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs).</strong></li>
<li><strong>Vesicoureteral Reflux.</strong></li>
</ul>
