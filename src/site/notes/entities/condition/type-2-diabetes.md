<h1>Type 2 Diabetes</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Type 2 diabetes is a chronic metabolic disease characterized by elevated blood sugar levels (hyperglycemia) primarily due to cellular insulin resistance and progressive loss of adequate pancreatic insulin secretion. It represents the vast majority of all diabetes cases globally and is strongly associated with obesity, poor diet, and sedentary lifestyle.</p>
