<h1>Acid-Base Imbalance</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>The body maintain a strict pH range. Imbalances are classified based on the cause: <strong>Respiratory</strong> (CO2 related) or <strong>Metabolic</strong> (Bicarbonate related). <strong>Metabolic Acidosis</strong> involves an excess of acid or loss of base, while <strong>Metabolic Alkalosis</strong> involve an excess of base or loss of acid.</p>
<h2>Types</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Metabolic Acidosis:</strong> Caused by kidney failure, DKA, or lactic acidosis.</li>
<li><strong>Metabolic Alkalosis:</strong> Caused by excessive vomiting or diuretic use.</li>
<li><strong>Respiratory Acidosis/Alkalosis:</strong> Caused by lung dysfunction affecting CO2 levels.</li>
</ul>
