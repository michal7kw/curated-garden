<h1>Postoperative Nausea and Vomiting</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Postoperative Nausea and Vomiting (PONV) is one of the most common and distressing complications of surgery and anesthesia. It affects about 30% of surgical patients but can be as high as 80% in high-risk groups. It can lead to dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, and wound dehiscence (surgical cut opening).</p>
<h2>Prevention</h2>
<p>Risk is assessed using the <strong>Apfel Score</strong>:</p>
<ol>
<li>Female gender.</li>
<li>Non-smoker.</li>
<li>History of PONV/motion sickness.</li>
<li>Postoperative opioids.</li>
</ol>
