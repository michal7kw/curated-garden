<h1>Polycystic Ovary Syndrome</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal imbalance that affects ovulation. The ovaries may develop numerous small collections of fluid (follicles) and fail to regularly release eggs. It is a leading cause of infertility.</p>
<h2>Complications</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Infertility.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Metabolic Syndrome:</strong> High blood pressure, high blood sugar, abnormal cholesterol.</li>
<li><strong>Type 2 Diabetes.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Sleep Apnea.</strong></li>
<li><strong>Endometrial Cancer.</strong></li>
</ul>
