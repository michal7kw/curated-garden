<h1>Nucleus</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Membrane-bound organelle containing the cell's genetic material (DNA). Controls gene expression, DNA replication, and RNA processing. Enclosed by a double nuclear envelope with nuclear pores for selective transport. Contains the nucleolus for ribosomal RNA synthesis.</p>
<h2>Structure</h2>
<ul>
<li>Double nuclear envelope with nuclear pore complexes</li>
<li>Chromatin (DNA + histone proteins)</li>
<li>Nucleolus (rRNA synthesis and ribosome assembly)</li>
<li>Nuclear lamina (structural support)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Key Functions</h2>
<ul>
<li>Houses and protects genomic DNA</li>
<li>Gene expression regulation (transcription)</li>
<li>DNA replication and repair</li>
<li>RNA processing (splicing, capping, polyadenylation)</li>
<li>Ribosome subunit assembly (nucleolus)</li>
</ul>
