<h1>Peroxisome</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Single-membrane organelle containing oxidative enzymes, primarily catalase and various oxidases. Specializes in very-long-chain fatty acid beta-oxidation, plasmalogen synthesis, and reactive oxygen species (ROS) detoxification. Plays a key role in lipid metabolism and cellular redox homeostasis.</p>
<h2>Structure</h2>
<ul>
<li>Single lipid bilayer membrane</li>
<li>Dense protein matrix containing catalase (up to 50% of peroxisomal protein)</li>
<li>Peroxins (PEX proteins) for biogenesis and protein import</li>
<li>Can proliferate by fission or de novo from ER</li>
</ul>
<h2>Key Functions</h2>
<ul>
<li>Very-long-chain fatty acid (VLCFA) beta-oxidation</li>
<li>ROS detoxification (catalase converts H2O2 → H2O + O2)</li>
<li>Plasmalogen synthesis (essential membrane lipids, especially in brain)</li>
<li>Bile acid synthesis</li>
<li>Purine catabolism and amino acid metabolism</li>
</ul>
