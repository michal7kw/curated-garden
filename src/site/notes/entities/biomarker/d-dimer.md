<h1>D-Dimer</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>D-Dimer is a fibrin degradation product released when cross-linked fibrin is broken down by plasmin, serving as a sensitive marker for the presence of intravascular clot formation and fibrinolysis.</p>
