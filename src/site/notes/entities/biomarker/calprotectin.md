<h1>Calprotectin</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Fecal calprotectin is a neutrophil-derived protein that serves as a non-invasive marker of gastrointestinal inflammation. It is the gold standard stool biomarker for distinguishing inflammatory bowel disease from irritable bowel syndrome.</p>
