<h1>PSA</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Prostate-specific antigen is a serine protease produced by prostatic epithelial cells. It is the primary screening biomarker for prostate cancer, though elevations also occur in benign prostatic hyperplasia and prostatitis.</p>
