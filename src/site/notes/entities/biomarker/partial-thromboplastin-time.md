<h1>Partial Thromboplastin Time (aPTT)</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Coagulation test measuring the time for citrated plasma to clot via the intrinsic and common coagulation pathways after addition of a partial thromboplastin (phospholipid) plus a contact activator (kaolin, silica, or ellagic acid). Sensitive to deficiencies of factors VIII, IX, XI, XII (intrinsic pathway) plus factors II, V, X, and fibrinogen (common pathway). Insensitive to factor VII deficiency (which only affects PT).</p>
<p>Primary monitoring tool for unfractionated heparin therapy (target: 1.5-2.5× ULN). Low-molecular-weight heparins (enoxaparin, dalteparin) are typically NOT monitored by aPTT — they affect anti-Xa activity more than aPTT.</p>
