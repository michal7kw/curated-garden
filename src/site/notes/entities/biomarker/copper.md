<h1>Copper</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Copper is an essential trace mineral required as a cofactor for numerous enzymes involved in energy production, iron metabolism, connective tissue synthesis, neurotransmitter synthesis, and antioxidant defense (superoxide dismutase). Both deficiency and excess are clinically significant.</p>
