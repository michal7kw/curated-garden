<h1>Yoga</h1>
<h2>Overview</h2>
<p>Mind-body practice combining physical postures (asana), breathwork (pranayama), and meditation. Evidence supports benefits for stress reduction, flexibility, balance, chronic pain, anxiety, and cardiovascular risk factors. Multiple styles range from gentle (Yin, Restorative) to vigorous (Ashtanga, Power Yoga).</p>
<h2>Mechanism of Action</h2>
<p>Combines physical loading (muscular activation, flexibility), autonomic regulation (via pranayama and sustained postures), and mindfulness. Reduces cortisol and sympathetic tone. Increases GABA levels in brain. Enhances vagal tone. Improves proprioception and balance. Anti-inflammatory effects via HPA axis modulation.</p>
<h2>Protocol</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Frequency</strong>: 2-5 sessions per week</li>
<li><strong>Duration</strong>: 30-60 minutes per session</li>
<li><strong>Styles</strong>: Hatha (general), Yin (deep flexibility), Vinyasa (fitness), Restorative (recovery)</li>
</ul>
